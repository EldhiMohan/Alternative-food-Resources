Vegan Lentil Soup


Ingredients:

  • 1 cup dried lentils
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 carrot, chopped
  • 1 celery stalk, chopped
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot. Add onions, garlic, carrots, and celery. Sauté for 5 minutes.
  2. Add lentils, diced tomatoes, and vegetable broth. Bring to a boil.
  3. Reduce heat and let it simmer for 30 minutes, or until lentils are tender.
  4. Season with salt and pepper, and serve warm.

Chickpea Salad


Ingredients:

  • 1 can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 red onion, diced
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine chickpeas, cucumber, bell pepper, and red onion.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, cumin, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss to combine. Serve chilled.

Tofu Stir-Fry


Ingredients:

  • 1 block tofu, cubed
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 broccoli crown, cut into florets
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, minced
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Press the tofu to remove excess water, then cut it into cubes.
  2. In a large skillet, heat sesame oil. Add tofu and sauté until golden brown on all sides.
  3. Add garlic, ginger, bell pepper, and broccoli. Stir-fry for 5-7 minutes.
  4. Stir in soy sauce, rice vinegar, and sesame seeds. Cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  5. Serve warm with rice or noodles.
