Edible Insects: A Sustainable Food Resource

Discover the potential of edible insects as an alternative food source, rich in protein and essential nutrients.

Crickets


Benefits:

  • High in protein (65% by weight)
  • Rich in vitamins B12 and B2 (riboflavin)
  • Low environmental footprint

Common Uses:

  • Protein powder for smoothies
  • Cricket flour for baking
  • Snack bars and chips

Mealworms


Benefits:

  • High in protein (50% by weight)
  • Rich in fiber and omega-3 fatty acids
  • Efficient feed conversion ratio

Common Uses:

  • Mealworm burgers
  • Mealworm snacks
  • Animal feed and pet food

Black Soldier Fly Larvae


Benefits:

  • High protein content (45% by weight)
  • Great for animal feed, and used in food supplements
  • Excellent for recycling organic waste

Common Uses:

  • Animal feed (poultry, fish, pigs)
  • Fertilizers
  • Probiotics in human food

Ants


Benefits:

  • Rich in protein and fat content
  • High in antioxidants
  • Can be eaten whole or ground into powder

Common Uses:

  • Ant-based seasonings
  • Ant protein powder
  • Used as a crunchy topping in dishes
