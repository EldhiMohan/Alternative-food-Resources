Examples of Plant-Based Foods

Learn about the nutritional benefits and versatility of plant-based foods.

Popular Plant-Based Foods

Lentils


Lentils are a great source of protein and fiber. They're rich in iron and folate, making them a popular choice in plant-based diets.

Chickpeas


Chickpeas are high in protein and can be used in a variety of dishes such as hummus, curries, or salads.

Tofu


Tofu is made from soybeans and is a versatile protein source. It can be grilled, fried, or used in smoothies and desserts.

Quinoa


Quinoa is a complete protein, meaning it contains all nine essential amino acids. It's gluten-free and packed with fiber and vitamins.

Avocados


Avocados are rich in healthy fats, particularly monounsaturated fats, which are good for heart health.
